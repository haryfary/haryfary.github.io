FAQ

Do I need an EORI to import into Germany?

Yes. We help you validate and complete the EORI process before your first shipment.

Can you estimate landed cost?

We calculate duties, freight, insurance, EUSt, and handling to give you a clear, itemized figure.

Which Incoterms do you recommend?

Depends on control vs. cost. For SMEs we often use FOB/FCA for control, DAP/DDP when simplicity matters.
