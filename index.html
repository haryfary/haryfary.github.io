FAQ

Do I need an EORI to import from India?

Yes. You’ll need an EORI before your first import. We’ll guide you through the process.

How do you estimate landed cost?

We combine HS duty rates, freight, insurance, import VAT (EUSt) and handling fees into a transparent breakdown.

Can you handle CE/REACH/RoHS?

We coordinate lab testing, documentation checks, and labeling reviews to meet EU requirements.

Which Incoterms do you recommend?

It depends on control vs cost. We frequently support FOB, FCA, DAP, and DDP scenarios.
